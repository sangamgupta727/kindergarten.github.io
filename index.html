About Us

Welcome to Golden Poppy Preschool,
Infant & Day Care

We are a privately owned, licensed and independent school. Our goal is to provide an enriching and nurturing environment with a focus on developmental activities.

Golden Poppy Preschool and Infant Center was founded in 1982, by Gail Reed, an educator and mother whose dream and vision was to establish a high caliber early childhood educational facility

  • Safe, Caring and Loving environment
  • Experienced & well trained Staff
  • Comprehensive program for 0-6 years old
  • Hot Lunches Served
  • Siblings discount offered

Our Programs

Infant Program

This Program Provides primary care and stimulating learning activities for babies who are not yet walking

Toddler Program

This Program help children’s function with more structure and direction, yet can still explore and play independently.

2 Year Old Program

This program introduces children to a more structured learning environment which focuses on self-confidence & peer interactions

3 Year Old Program

In this program Children are grouped by age & maturity level & are introduced to learning activities & developmental milestones

Pre K Program

This Program Includes knowledge of personal information, recognition letters, understanding of phonics, recognition of numbers

Transitional Kindergarten

The program focuses on gross motor, fine motor, cognitive, language, behavioral and self-help skills through hands on learning.
